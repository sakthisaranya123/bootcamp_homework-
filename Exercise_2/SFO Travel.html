Explore San Francisco

Your guide to the best of the Bay Area

Must-Visit Places in San Francisco

1. Golden Gate Bridge

The iconic Golden Gate Bridge offers breathtaking views of the city and the bay. Take a stroll or bike ride across this landmark.

2. Alcatraz Island

Explore the historic Alcatraz Island, home to the infamous Alcatraz Federal Penitentiary. Don't miss the guided tours for a captivating experience.

3. Fisherman's Wharf

Indulge in the vibrant atmosphere of Fisherman's Wharf. Enjoy fresh seafood, street performers, and visit attractions like Pier 39 and the Aquarium of the Bay.

Things to Do

  • Take a cable car ride through the city's hilly streets.
  • Visit the beautiful Golden Gate Park and explore its museums and gardens.
  • Discover the diverse neighborhoods like Chinatown, Little Italy, and the Mission District.
  • Sample local cuisine at the Ferry Building Marketplace.
  • Relax at the beaches, such as Baker Beach and Ocean Beach.

Itinerary

Day Activities
Day 1 Explore Golden Gate Bridge and nearby attractions. Enjoy dinner at Fisherman's Wharf.
Day 2 Take a ferry to Alcatraz Island. Afternoon visit to Pier 39 and the Aquarium of the Bay.
Day 3 Discover neighborhoods like Chinatown and Little Italy. Evening cable car ride.
Day 4 Relax at Golden Gate Park. Explore museums and gardens. Visit the Ferry Building Marketplace for dinner.
